## Projects

### Auto Insurance Claim Risk Analysis
Power BI dashboard analyzing driver demographics and behavior to identify factors associated with insurance claim risk.

Key Insights:
- Drivers aged 16–25 exhibit the highest claim rates (~72%).
- Claim probability decreases significantly as driving experience increases.
- Lower income segments show higher claim frequencies.
- Annual mileage shows a positive correlation with claim risk.

Data Visualization
