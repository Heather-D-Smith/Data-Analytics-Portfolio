## Featured Projects

### Auto Insurance Claim Risk Analysis

Power BI dashboard analyzing driver demographics and behavior to identify key factors that influence insurance claim risk.

**Project Workflow**

1. Raw dataset cleaned and structured using **Excel**
2. Data imported into **Power BI**
3. Interactive dashboard created to analyze claim risk factors
4. Visualizations used to identify high-risk driver segments

**Key Insights**

• Drivers aged **16–25** show the highest claim rates  
• Claim probability **decreases significantly with driving experience**  
• Lower income groups exhibit **higher claim frequencies**  
• Annual mileage shows a **positive correlation with claim risk**

The dashboard includes:

- Claim rate segmentation by age
- Risk comparison across driving experience levels
- Claim distribution analysis
- Risk correlation with annual mileage
- Income-based risk comparison

**Tools Used**

Power BI | Excel | Data Visualization | Exploratory Data Analysis
